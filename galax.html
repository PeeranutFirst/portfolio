Game Information

Galax Hero is a work-in-progress RPG Shoot-em-up (shmup) hybrid for the Android platform made by me using Godot Engine. This game is inspired by Shoot-em-up games like Star Force, 1942, and Gun Nac. In the game, the player pilots a spaceship while shooting various enemies. The game features Superpower where every successful hit made by the playerâ€™s projectile increases superpower gauge, allowing the ship to unleash the powerful ability when itâ€™s full. In addition, every time an enemy is destroyed, it creates multiple debris that spread and damages nearby enemies, allowing the creation of very devastating combos.
